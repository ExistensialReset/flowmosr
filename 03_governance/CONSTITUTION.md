# PREAMBLE

Flow exists to guarantee biological dignity, structural stability, and voluntary participation.

It does not replace culture.
It does not erase alternatives.
It does not centralize power.

Flow defines:

• A global Baseline floor
• A surplus circulation mechanism
• A rotating governance model
• A layered recovery structure
• A voluntary participation boundary

Human dignity is protected through invariants.
Power is stabilized through rotation.
Resources circulate to prevent extraction.

This Constitution defines the hierarchy and relationship between all core protocols.
