# 🗳️ Flow Governance
## Coordination without control

This folder defines how decisions are made in Flow.

Not through power.  
Not through authority.

But through **structured, non-coercive coordination**.

---

## 🧭 What this is

Governance in Flow is not leadership.

It is:
- Temporary responsibility
- Distributed participation
- Structured decision processes

---

## 🌸 Core mechanism: LOTUS

Governance is primarily handled through:

- Random selection  
- Rotation  
- Short mandates  

This prevents:
- Power accumulation  
- Political identity formation  
- Strategic manipulation  

---

## ⚖️ What governance must ensure

- No one gains control over others’ survival  
- Decisions can be revised  
- Harm is addressed without domination  

---

## 📂 What you will find here

- LOTUS protocols  
- Council structures  
- Conflict resolution systems  
- Exit and boundary protocols

## 🌊 Important

Governance is not there to control people.

It exists to:

> Protect the conditions that allow people to live freely.

---

## 💙 A small reminder

The goal is not to make perfect decisions.

The goal is to make decisions  
**without creating power**.
