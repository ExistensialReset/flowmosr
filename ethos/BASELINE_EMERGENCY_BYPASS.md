# BASELINE_EMERGENCY_BYPASS.md

**Version:** 1.2  
**Status:** OPERATIONAL SAFEGUARD  
**Authors:** Elinor Frejd, Claude & ChatGPT  

**Purpose:** Guarantee immediate protection of Baseline during threats or protocol delays, ensuring human verification, proportionality, and documentation.

---

## 1. ACTIVATION CONDITIONS

* Immediate threat to essential Baseline resources: water, food, medical care, shelter.  
* Widespread or acute psychological distress in one or more Nodes.  
* Ecosystem risks that could compromise Baseline within 6 months.  
* Mass panic, social collapse, or coordinated disruption scenarios.  
* **All activations require human verification, named decision-makers, and documented rationale.**

---

## 2. PROCEDURE

* **Resource distribution** may be initiated to protect Baseline, but actions are advisory until human authorization is recorded.  
* **Parallel investigation** continues according to protocol requirements.  
* **AI-assisted triage** may support detection and prioritization but cannot trigger irreversible action without human confirmation.  
* Post-bypass accountability: **regional council review within 30 days**, with documented outcomes.  
* **All steps must be documented**, including rationale, decision-makers, and any deviations.

---

## 3. TEMPORAL AND PROPORTIONALITY SAFEGUARDS

* Time windows (e.g., 24 hours) are operational targets, not automatic triggers.  
* Temporary actions must have **defined start and end times** and clear criteria for escalation or de-escalation.  
* Proportionality must be assessed for all resource distributions or emergency measures.  

---

## 4. PSYCHOLOGICAL AND ECOSYSTEM MONITORING

* If **>30% of Nodes report acute psychological distress**, the system flags for human review.  
* Any activation of a formal “mental health emergency” requires **documented human verification** and named decision-makers.  
* Ecosystem damage threatening Baseline triggers an **ecological emergency protocol**, only after independent review and documented rationale.  

---

## 5. SCALABILITY TIERS

* **Minor (<10% Baseline impact):** Remote verification, 1-2 humans, AI advisory.  
* **Medium (10–50%):** Hybrid AI + 2–3 humans, remote & in-person, human decision-making required.  
* **Major (>50%):** Full in-person team (5–7 humans), external oversight, documented proportional plan.  

---

## 6. INTEGRATION

* Tightly coupled with **PROTOCOL_PRIORITY_MATRIX.md** to avoid conflicts.  
* Activations feed into **RESOURCE_METRIC_STANDARDS.md** in `/core` for L×S×I and Baseline monitoring.  
* All AI outputs are advisory; **no automated escalation or irreversible action** without human approval.  

---

## 7. DOCUMENTATION AND OVERSIGHT

* All actions, deviations, and outcomes must be logged.  
* Named human decision-makers must sign off for emergency activation, adjustments, or escalations.  
* Independent review must occur within 30 days for all activated bypasses.  
* All lessons learned are incorporated into regional protocol updates.
