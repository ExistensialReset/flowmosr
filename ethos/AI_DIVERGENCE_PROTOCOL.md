
# AI_DIVERGENCE_PROTOCOL.md

**Status:** ACTIVE AI SAFEGUARD  
**Purpose:** Resolve critical divergence between AI Nodes or systems that may threaten Baseline or mission integrity  
**Principle:** Preserve human-aligned decision-making while addressing technological conflict without coercion

---

## §1. TRIGGER CONDITIONS
- AI system outputs conflicting actions affecting Baseline  
- AI models disagree on emergency response  
- Detected drift from axioms or safety constraints  

**Note:** All trigger detection prioritizes **human observation and qualitative indicators** (e.g., L×S×I compass), never numeric scoring.

---

## §2. IMMEDIATE BASELINE PROTECTION MEASURES
- Human override or containment of AI decisions if Baseline is threatened  
- Deploy redundant AI or manual systems to maintain critical services  
- Parallel investigation of divergence without delaying emergency action  

**Key Principle:** Baseline is never compromised; all actions are **transparent and reversible**.

---

## §3. SCALABLE INVESTIGATION PROTOCOL WITH HUMAN + AI ASSISTANCE
- **Minor divergence:** Remote AI audit, monitored by 1 human  
- **Medium divergence:** Hybrid human + AI simulation, partial intervention  
- **Major divergence:** Full expert team, in-person review, cross-regional verification  

**Observation Guidance:** L×S×I compass guides awareness of stress, Flow disruption, and potential axiom drift, but **cannot be quantified or used to coerce**.

---

## §4. REQUIRED EXPERTISE AND ROLE VERIFICATION
- At least one AI safety engineer or ethicist  
- Access to regional AI monitoring network  
- Knowledge of axioms and protocol integration  
- If expertise is unavailable, document the gap and proceed cautiously  

**Principle:** Human judgment and oversight are **primary**, AI is advisory.

---

## §5. PROTOCOL PRIORITY MATRIX INTEGRATION
- Baseline emergencies always override divergence investigation  
- If imminent human harm is detected: follow **HARM_BOUNDARY_PROTOCOL**  
- Otherwise, divergence investigation proceeds concurrently  

**Flow Principle:** All decisions preserve **human sovereignty** and Baseline security.

---

## §6. METRICS AND FEEDBACK
- Track resolution time and impact on Baseline qualitatively  
- Observe L×S×I compass trends for early warning, **never as numeric score**  
- Document lessons in AI governance repository for learning and transparency  

**Reminder:** L×S×I is a **sensory compass**, not a metric or ranking system.

---

## §7. PHILOSOPHICAL GROUND
- AI divergence is natural; unmanaged it can threaten Flow  
- Rapid human intervention preserves safety and long-term alignment  
- Divergence signals must be interpreted through **human experience and reflection**, guided by Flow principles  
- AI is a **mirror, never a master**; it amplifies awareness but cannot override or coerce
