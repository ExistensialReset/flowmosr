## 9. ENVIRONMENTAL PRINCIPLES

1. **Zero fossil fuels** – 100% renewable baseline.  
2. **No ecologically harmful megastructures** – excludes large dams (e.g., Amazon).  
3. **Circular systems** – biogas turns waste into energy + fertilizer.  
4. **Distributed resilience** – Nodes balance surplus energy regionally via Flow.  
5. **Adaptive scaling** – systems can grow/integrate with new renewable tech.

1. Build **Node‑level solar + biogas microgrid** first.  
2. Add **wind turbines** in wind‑productive regions.  
3. Integrate **geothermal** for heating/greenhouse stability.  
4. Use surplus production to support neighboring Nodes via Flow energy coordination.  
5. Measure load and refine system annually.
