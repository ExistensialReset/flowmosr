## 4. Energy Production & Storage

| Node Type      | Production (kWh/year) | Storage (kWh) | Notes |
|---------------|----------------------|---------------|-------|
| Urban         | 416,100             | 50            | Solar PV 50 kWh/day, battery backup 50 kWh |
| Peri-Urban    | 1,095,000           | 100           | Solar PV + small wind, battery 100 kWh |
| Regional      | 2,190,000           | 500           | Solar 100×50 kWh/day + Wind 50×20 kWh/day, battery storage 500 kWh |

**Notes on Storage:**  
- Batteries sized to cover daily fluctuations and short-term cloudy/windless periods.  
- Can also serve to provide surplus energy to adjacent nodes.  
- Verkningsgrad ~90%, degraderar ca 2%/år.
