## 1. Purpose

This document provides a **comprehensive global cost model for hydroponic food production**, including:

- CAPEX and OPEX per Node (500 m²)
- Annualized costs
- Yield per crop
- Energy & water consumption
- Translation of monetary cost into **labor hours** per crop
- Regional variations for Europe, North America, South America, Asia, Oceania, North Africa, and Sub-Saharan Africa

It is fully traceable: all calculations and assumptions are shown.

## 7. Cost per kg and Labor Hours per kg

- Labor Hours/kg = Annual Labor Hours / Crop Yield (assuming proportional allocation to crop by revenue or priority)
