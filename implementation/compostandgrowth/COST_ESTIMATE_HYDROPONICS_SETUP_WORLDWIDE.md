## 1. PURPOSE

All costs are in **USD** and reflect active commercial ranges.

## 5. SCALE NOTES

1. **Smaller setups (<500 m²):** setup can be $100k–$250k due to simpler infrastructure needs.  
2. **Larger setups (1,000–3,000 m²):** may exceed $500k and reach $1M+ depending on automation and climate systems. Markets show commercial vertical farms often invest hundreds of thousands to multimillion USD in CAPEX9.

## 6. SUMMARY

Hydroponic setup costs are **non‑trivial but scalable**:

- **Benchmark range:** approximately **$150,000 to $425,000 per Node** globally at commercial scale.  
- Variations depend on region, automation level, and facility complexity.

This cost focuses on **long‑term infrastructure**, not consumables or ongoing operating expenses.
