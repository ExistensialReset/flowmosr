## 1. PURPOSE

This document provides **realistic Node-level setup and annual operating costs** for hydroponic production worldwide. Costs include **capital setup**, amortized over 5 years, and **annual operating expenses**, adjusted for regional labour, energy, and materials.  

---

## 2. ASSUMPTIONS

- Node: 500 m² vertical hydroponic system  
- Crops: Leafy greens, herbs, microalgae, hydroponic lentils, tomatoes, basil  
- System type: NFT/DWC + LED lighting  
- Setup amortized over 5 years  
- Costs in **USD**, realistic market data  

---
