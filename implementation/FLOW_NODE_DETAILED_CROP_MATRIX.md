# 🗂️ FLOW NODE DETAILED CROP MATRIX (APPENDIX A)

**Purpose:** Provide operational planning data for daily cultivation, including energy, water, labor, and cost per crop.  
**Scope:** Urban node example (values averaged over the year).  
**Note:** Actual production varies with season, light, and node conditions. Labor values per crop only include direct cultivation; total node labor includes overhead, maintenance, and administration.

## Notes & Clarifications

1. **Daily figures are averages** – actual production varies by season, sunlight, temperature, and node conditions.  
2. **Labor per crop** includes only direct cultivation activities (seeding, harvesting, monitoring).  
3. **Total node labor** includes overhead, maintenance, administration, and unplanned activities – sum of crop labor ≠ total node labor.  
4. **Daily cost** includes CAPEX amortization and annual OPEX, averaged across the year.  
5. **Resource planning:** Tables can be used for scheduling irrigation, energy allocation, and labor assignments on a daily basis.  

---

## Usage Example

- To know the cost of producing **11 kg of lettuce** daily:  
  - **Energy:** 70 kWh  
  - **Water:** 600 L  
  - **Labor:** 0.5 hours  
  - **Cost:** $79  

- Aggregate for multiple crops to estimate **daily node consumption and labor requirements**.
