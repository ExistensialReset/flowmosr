## 1. PURPOSE

This document outlines the **comprehensive organization of healthcare in Flow**.  
It covers:

- Decentralized, multi-level healthcare Nodes  
- Green Labs production of all **medicines, hygiene, and cleaning products**  
- Availability of **all types of care and medical aids**  
- Resource flow, buffers, and environmental sustainability  
- Realistic cost estimates per Node    

---

## 2. PRINCIPLES

- **Decentralized yet coordinated**: Each Node provides primary care; regional Nodes provide specialist & emergency care.  
- **Self-sufficient resources**: Green Labs produce medicine, hygiene, cleaning products locally or regionally.  
- **Environmental consciousness**: 100% renewable energy, biodegradable products, sustainable transport.  
- **Digital and AI integration**: Telemedicine, monitoring, AI-assisted triage.  
- **Universal accessibility**: Children, elderly, disabled, and all medical needs considered.  
- **Preventive care focus**: Nutrition, exercise, mental health, and hygiene to reduce acute interventions.  

---

## 3. NODE TYPES AND FUNCTIONS

| Node Type                     | Main Functions                                                                 |
|-------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Primary Care Node**          | Health checks, vaccinations, minor illnesses, AI triage                        |
| **Specialist Care Node**       | Specialist consultation, lab diagnostics, telemedicine integration            |
| **Emergency / Surgical Node**  | Emergency interventions, surgeries, trauma care                                |
| **Rehabilitation Node**        | Physical therapy, neurological rehab, mobility support, neurofeedback         |
| **Regional Green Lab**         | Production of all medicines, hygiene, cleaning products; distribution control |

---

## 4. GREEN LABS PRODUCTION

- **Medicines:** All types – primary, specialist, emergency, palliative  
- **Hygiene products:** Soap, hand sanitizer, toothpaste, menstrual products – 100% biodegradable  
- **Cleaning products:** Enzyme and plant-based, fully biodegradable  
- **Medical aids:** Wheelchairs, monitors, respirators, rehab equipment  
- **Environmental design:** Energy self-sufficient, minimal waste, locally or regionally modular  

---

## 5. RESOURCE FLOW

- **Flow distribution:** AI coordinates Green Labs production & delivers to Nodes based on usage and buffer levels  
- **Buffer stock per Node:** Critical medicines, hygiene, cleaning products always stocked  
- **Transport:** Renewable energy, solar/wind/biogas powered drones and vehicles  
- **Redundancy:** Regional Green Labs can cover high-demand Nodes rapidly
