# 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Flow Facilitators
## Holding space without controlling it

This folder defines the role of facilitators in Flow.

Not leaders.  
Not authorities.  

But **temporary holders of process and safety**.

---

## 🧭 What this is

Facilitators exist to:

- Maintain clarity in group processes  
- Support communication  
- Protect psychological and relational safety  

They do not:
- Make decisions for others  
- Hold power  
- Define outcomes  

---

## ⚖️ Core constraint

> A facilitator must never become necessary.

If a system depends on a person to function:

→ It is not stable  
→ It must be redesigned  

---

## 🔄 Role characteristics

- Rotational  
- Voluntary  
- Transparent  
- Replaceable  

---

## 🧠 What lives here

- Facilitation protocols  
- Witness roles (e.g. Refugium Anima)  
- Conflict support structures  
- Group process guidance  

---

## 🌊 Important

Facilitation is not influence.

It is:

> Protecting the conditions where people can think and act freely.

---

## 💙 A small reminder

The best facilitation is invisible.

When it works, no one feels led.
