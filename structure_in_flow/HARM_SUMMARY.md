# HARM_SUMMARY.md

**Status:** Draft  
**Scope:** All Flow Nodes, Spirals, and participants  
**Purpose:** Provide a concise, actionable summary of the UNFORGIVABLE HARM Protocol for quick reference, ensuring clarity on definitions, boundaries, and mandatory Node actions.

---

## 1. Core Principle

- **The victim never moves. The perpetrator always moves.**  
- Flow tolerates all emotions, all failure, all struggle.  
- Flow does **not** tolerate sadism, violence, or abuse.

---

## 2. Definition of Unforgivable Harm

Unforgivable harm is defined as **intentional and systemic boundary violations** via force, malice, or sustained harassment.  

### 2.1 Sadistic Harm
- Intentional infliction of suffering for perpetrator’s satisfaction.  
- Sustained pattern despite clear communication and requests to stop.  
- No attempt at repair or restoration of trust.  

### 2.2 Physical and Weaponized Violence
- Any use of weapons (guns, knives, etc.) against persons or vulnerable beings.  
- Immediate escalation to Node emergency protocol.

### 2.3 Abuse of Authority / Manipulation
- Coercion, gaslighting, or sustained intimidation.  
- Violation of consent, repeated despite warnings.

---

## 3. Mandatory Node Actions

1. **Immediate Acknowledgment**  
   - Node receives report → confirm receipt within SLA (max 24h).  

2. **Initial Assessment**  
   - Evaluate report for credibility, severity, and corroboration.  
   - Preserve anonymity if requested.  

3. **Containment / Protective Measures**  
   - Victim safety first: isolate perpetrator influence where possible.  
   - Deploy temporary LOTUS panel if Node lacks capacity.  

4. **Verification & Documentation**  
   - Collect evidence, witness statements, and logs.  
   - Use encrypted off-chain storage; generate hash for audit trail.  

5. **Escalation**  
   - High-risk or urgent cases → immediate LOTUS lottery panel assignment.  
   - Follow conflict escalation paths if interpersonal/social dynamics involved.  

6. **Follow-Up & Audit**  
   - Ensure remediation or separation actions are completed.  
   - Node records action hashes on-chain for transparency and audit.

---

## 4. Quick Reference Matrix

| Harm Type | Action Required | Escalation | SLA |
|-----------|----------------|------------|-----|
| Sadistic Harm | Verify & protect | LOTUS Panel | 24h |
| Physical/Weaponized | Emergency containment | LOTUS Panel + Law | Immediate |
| Authority Abuse | Isolate influence | Conflict Path & LOTUS | 48h |
| Threats / Coercion | Assess & mitigate | Node + LOTUS | 24h |

---

## 5. Notes

- **Anonymity:** Always respect reporter anonymity; PII only for internal Node use under strict encryption.  
- **Audit:** Every step must be logged with immutable hash references for future verification.  
- **Conflict Overlap:** If harm intersects with other conflicts, prioritize victim protection first.
