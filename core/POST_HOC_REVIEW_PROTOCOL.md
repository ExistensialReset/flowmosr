# POST_HOC_REVIEW_PROTOCOL.md

**Status:** ACTIVE  
**Layer:** Governance Correction  
**Trigger:** Any sanctioned action  

---

# 1. PURPOSE

Ensure decisions remain proportional and legitimate over time.

This protocol prevents structural rigidity.

---

# 2. TRIGGER

Post-hoc review automatically initiates when:

- Sanction duration ends
- Mandate period closes
- Procedural anomaly detected

---

# 3. REVIEW BODY

- New LOTUS-selected panel  
- No overlap with original decision-makers  

---

# 4. REVIEW CRITERIA

- Evidence integrity  
- Proportionality  
- Procedural compliance  
- Baseline impact  

---

# 5. POSSIBLE OUTCOMES

## Decision upheld  
Archived with validation log.

## Decision disproportionate  
Adjustment required; corrective support issued.

## Procedural error  
Decision voided; system correction documented.

---

# 6. TRANSPARENCY LEVEL

Public summary required.  
Sensitive operational data may be anonymized.

---

# 7. PRINCIPLE

Process legitimacy is preserved through correction — not punishment.
