# 📊 FLOW PILOT DATA TEMPLATE v1.0

**Purpose:** Generate real-world data from first Micro-Circles  
**Applies to:** 2–10 people  
**Duration:** Minimum 12 weeks  
**Principle:** Measure structure, not morality.

---

## 0. BASELINE SNAPSHOT (Before Week 1)

Each participant (private, optional to share):

- Monthly essential costs: ______
- Monthly income: ______
- Perceived stress level (1–10): ______
- Perceived loneliness (1–10): ______
- Perceived agency (1–10): ______
- Hours worked per week: ______
- Hours of real rest per week: ______

Circle records only aggregated averages.

---

## 1. ATTENDANCE STABILITY

Weekly:
- Planned meetings: ___
- Actual meetings held: ___
- Average attendance rate: ___ %

If attendance <60% for 4 weeks → structural issue.

---

## 2. RESOURCE FLOW TRACKING

Track only measurable transfers.

| Week | Type | Value (est.) | Notes |
|------|------|--------------|-------|
| 1 | Food | 400 kr | Bulk rice shared |
| 2 | Labor | 3 hrs | Bike repair |
| 3 | Tool | 1 drill | Borrowed 2 days |

End of 12 weeks:
- Total estimated shared value: ______
- % of members' monthly baseline covered: ______

Target after 12 weeks:
5–10% baseline substitution.

---

## 3. CONFLICT EVENTS

Track structurally, not emotionally.

- Number of conflicts: ___
- Resolved via Pause-Name-Return: ___
- Required third party: ___
- Led to exit: ___

If >3 unresolved conflicts → governance weakness.

---

## 4. VOLUNTARY EXIT RATE

- Members at start: ___
- Members at week 12: ___
- Voluntary exits: ___
- Stated reason categories:
  - Time constraint
  - Emotional overload
  - Financial tension
  - Ideological mismatch
  - Other

Exit rate >40% → structural misalignment.

---

## 5. PSYCHOLOGICAL SHIFT (Week 12)

Re-measure:

- Stress (1–10)
- Loneliness (1–10)
- Agency (1–10)
- Hours worked
- Hours rest

Compare to Week 0.

We are looking for directional change, not perfection.

---

## 6. RESILIENCE TEST (Mini)

Simulated scenario:
"If 2 members lost income tomorrow, could Circle support them for 1 month?"

Answer:
- Yes / Partially / No

Document why.

---

## 7. SELF-ASSESSMENT

Circle answers:

- Did pressure increase?
- Did coercion appear?
- Did hierarchy start forming?
- Did someone become informal leader?
- Did documentation decline?
- Did joy increase?

---

## SUCCESS CRITERIA (Micro-Pilot)

After 12 weeks:

✓ 70%+ attendance  
✓ 5–10% baseline substitution  
✓ Conflict resolution functioning  
✓ Stress not increased  
✓ Exit rate <40%  

If achieved → eligible for Phase 2 (Economic Coordination).
