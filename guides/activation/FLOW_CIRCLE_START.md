## 🌍 Start a Flow Circle (Anywhere in the World)

> **Flow begins wherever you are.**

You do not need permission, funding, or perfect conditions to start.

A **Flow Circle** is the first step toward a Node:
- 3–10 people
- Meeting regularly
- Exploring Flow principles in practice
- Building trust before infrastructure

---

### 🚀 How to Start

1. Gather 2–5 people  
2. Meet (in person or online)  
3. Share a meal or conversation  
4. Ask: *"What do we actually need to feel okay?"*  
5. Start helping each other — no transactions  

That’s it. You’ve started.

---

### 🧭 What a Circle Does

- Builds trust and relationships  
- Studies Flow documents together  
- Maps skills and needs  
- Experiments with no-money cooperation  
- Prepares for potential Node creation  

**No pressure to scale. No obligation to succeed.**

### 🌊 Principle

> **Flow is not launched. It emerges.**

Every Circle is a seed.

Some will grow into Nodes.  
Some will dissolve.  
Some will transform into something unexpected.

**All of them matter.**
