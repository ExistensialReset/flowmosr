# FLOW FINAL PRIORITY LAYER (FPL)

1. **Baseline survival overrides all** (water, food, safety, immediate stability).  
2. **CRISIS applies only during active crisis** to rapidly stabilize the system.  
3. **RESOURCE applies in stable conditions** for fair allocation (dialogue/simulation/lottery).  
4. **CEPP applies when no crisis is active**, ensuring autonomy and exit rights.  
5. **All CRISIS overrides are temporary, logged, and must revert after stabilization.**
