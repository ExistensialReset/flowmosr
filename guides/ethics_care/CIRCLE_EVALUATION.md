## 🌊 Circles L × S × I Reflection — Gentle Check-In

> **No scores. No pressure. Always safe.**  
> **Refugium Anima is always open.** Even if there isn’t a physical sanctuary, the circle creates a space to pause, rest, or just be.

### L × S × I in the Circle
- **L — Lugn (Calm):** Are bodies and minds feeling safe, steady, and held?  
- **S — Spontanitet (Spontaneity):** Can everyone act, play, or choose freely?  
- **I — Inkännande (Empathy):** Do people feel seen, heard, and supported?

### How We Reflect
- Share stories, draw moods, or write notes  
- Take turns gently facilitating  
- Observations guide care, not judgement  
- If someone struggles, create **an immediate Refugium Anima** — a safe space to collapse, scream, weep, or just breathe  

### Remember
- There’s no “good” or “bad” here  
- Reflection is **for humans, by humans**  
- The circle nurtures calm, play, and empathy — always with care at the center
