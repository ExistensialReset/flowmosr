---
## 📌 WHAT THIS IS

This is the shortest possible way to start Flow. No theory. No big words. Just seven days of very small actions.

If you have water, food, or safety crises – start here anyway. Doing one small thing together builds the trust you need to face the bigger things later.

---

## 🚀 FIRST 7 DAYS PROTOCOL
### How to Start Flow (Without Calling It Flow)

**Purpose:**  
Turn 0 → Level 0 in 7 days  
No money required  
No theory required  

**Rule:**  
Don’t explain Flow. Just do simple things together.

---

## DAY 1: FIND YOUR PEOPLE

**Goal:** 2–5 people who trust each other (even a little)

**What to do:**
- Talk to neighbors, family, or friends  
- Ask simple question:  
  > “What is hardest in your daily life right now?”  

**Listen for:**
- Water
- Food
- Childcare
- Time
- Safety

**Do NOT:**
- Pitch ideas
- Explain systems
- Use big words

**End of Day 1:**
You have a small group willing to try something together

---

## DAY 2: AGREE ON ONE SMALL THING

**Goal:** One shared action (very small)

**Ask:**
> “What is ONE thing we can make easier if we do it together?”

**Pick ONLY ONE:**
- Share meals  
- Fetch water together  
- Watch children together  
- Share tools  

**Rule:**
If it feels big → make it smaller

**End of Day 2:**
Clear agreement:
- What
- When
- Who

---

## DAY 3: DO IT ONCE

**Goal:** First real action

Examples:
- One shared meal  
- One water trip together  
- One shared childcare session  

**Important:**
- Keep it simple  
- Don’t optimize  
- Don’t judge  

**After:**
Ask:
> “Did this make today easier?”

---

## DAY 4: REPEAT + ADJUST

**Goal:** Do it again (slightly better)

- Repeat same action  
- Fix small problems  

Examples:
- Cooking rotation clearer  
- Time adjusted  
- Roles clearer  

**Important:**
Improvement > perfection

---

## DAY 5: ADD SECOND LAYER

**Goal:** Add ONE more shared action

Only if first one works.

Examples:
- If sharing meals → add childcare  
- If water → add tool sharing  

**Rule:**
Never add more than one thing at a time

---

## DAY 6: CREATE SIMPLE ROTATION

**Goal:** Avoid hidden hierarchy early

**Do:**
- Decide rotation (who does what, when)  
- Write it OR remember clearly  

**Optional:**
- Draw lots if disagreement  

**Important:**
No one becomes “the one who always does it”

---

## DAY 7: FIRST CIRCLE MOMENT

**Goal:** Reflect together

Sit together (even 20–30 min)

Ask:

1. What became easier this week?  
2. What was difficult?  
3. Should we continue?  

Then:

- Keep what works  
- Drop what doesn’t  
- Adjust roles if needed  

---

## END OF WEEK 1 RESULT

If successful, you now have:

- Shared daily/weekly action  
- Increased trust  
- Time saved (even 1–2 hours matters)  
- A functioning micro-circle  

**This is Level 0.**

---

## ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES

### 1. Starting too big
→ Fix: Make it smaller  

### 2. Talking too much
→ Fix: Act first, talk after  

### 3. One person taking control
→ Fix: Rotate immediately  

### 4. Trying to help everyone
→ Fix: Start with 2–5 people only  

---

## 🔑 KEY INSIGHT

Flow does not start with systems.

It starts when:
> “We do one thing together that makes life easier today.”

Everything else comes later.

---

## WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

If this works:

- Week 2–4 → stabilize Level 0  
- Month 1–3 → move to water (Level 1)  

If it doesn’t:

- Try again with smaller group  
- Try simpler action  
- Try different people  

---

## FINAL NOTE

If people feel:
- Less stress  
- More time  
- More support  

They will continue.

If not:
They won’t.

**That is the only metric that matters in the beginning.**
