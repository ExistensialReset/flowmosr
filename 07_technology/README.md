# 🧠 Flow Technology
## Tools that serve life — not control it

This folder defines how technology exists within Flow.

Not as a driver of behavior.  
Not as a system of control.  

But as a **support layer for life**.

---

## 🧭 What this is

Technology in Flow is not neutral.

It either:
- Supports life  
- Or distorts it  

There is no middle ground.

---

## ⚙️ What lives here

This folder contains:

### 🔧 Technology Principles
Core constraints for all technological systems:
- No coercion through design  
- No dependency traps  
- No extraction of human attention or behavior  

Technology must remain:
- Transparent  
- Understandable  
- Replaceable  

---

### 🤖 AI Principles
Defines how AI can exist without becoming a control structure.

AI in Flow:
- Does not manipulate  
- Does not optimize humans  
- Does not accumulate power  

Instead, it:
- Assists understanding  
- Supports coordination  
- Translates complexity into clarity  

---

## ⚖️ Core constraint

> Technology must never become something humans depend on to survive.

If it does, it becomes power.

And must be redesigned or removed.

---

## 🧠 How to read this

Start with:
→ Technology principles  

Then:
→ AI principles  

Then explore implementation and edge cases.

---

## 🌊 Important

Technology is not the foundation of Flow.

It is a **layer on top of human life**.

If it disappears, life must still function.

---

## 💙 A small reminder

The goal is not advanced technology.

The goal is:

> A world where technology is no longer needed to feel okay.
