# FLOW COST SUMMARY (Quick Reference)

**Scope:** Universal Baseline for 8.2B people  
**Author:** Claude + Elinor  
**Location:** /data_validation

---

## 1️⃣ Key Numbers

| Metric | Amount | Notes |
|--------|-------|------|
| **Total CAPEX** | $51.1T | Spread 20-30 years, includes housing, water, energy, food nodes |
| **Total OPEX** | $4.67T/year | $1.56/person/day |
| **Current global waste** | $14T/year | Fossil subsidies, military, marketing, planned obsolescence, food waste |
| **Net surplus after Flow** | $9-10T/year | If waste redirected |
| **Payback timeline** | 12-22 years | Self-funding through recovered waste |

## 2️⃣ Phased Implementation

| Phase | Population | CAPEX | OPEX/year | Waste Recovery | Outcome |
|-------|-----------|-------|------------|----------------|---------|
| **Phase 1 (0-5y)** | 10M | $61B | $5.7B | $20B | Pilot, workforce training, supply chain setup |

## 3️⃣ Physical Feasibility

- **Nodes needed:** 16.4M (500 ppl/node)  
- **Construction rate:** 547,000 Nodes/year → ~1,500 Nodes/day globally  
- **Materials scaling:** 1.5-3x current production (steel, concrete, solar, wind)  
- **Workforce:** 16M additional needed; training scalable  

---

## 4️⃣ Environmental Impact

- CO₂ reduction: 50-55% of current emissions  
- Net emissions: 6-8 GtCO₂/year remaining  
- Timeline to net-zero: 15-25 years post full Flow deployment  

---

## 5️⃣ Political & Economic Context

- **Barrier:** Not technical, material, or workforce  
- **Main obstacle:** Political vested interests (~$13.5T/year in subsidies & spending)  
- **Opportunity:** Redirect current waste → self-funding, ROI positive  

---

## 6️⃣ Quick Insights

- We **already spend enough money**; waste is the problem  
- CAPEX looks huge until compared to current fossil subsidies ($7.2T/year)  
- Phased rollout allows self-funding and gradual adoption  
- Even **partial adoption** has measurable benefits  
- Flow is **possible**, **affordable**, **physically feasible**  
- Political will and coordination are the real challenge
