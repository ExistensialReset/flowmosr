# 🔬 Flow Simulations
## Testing reality before building it

This folder contains simulations of Flow in practice.

Not as speculation.  
But as **stress tests of reality**.

---

## 🧭 What this is

Simulations explore:

- What happens when Flow is applied  
- Where it breaks  
- What needs to adapt  

They are not “visions”.

They are **controlled approximations of real conditions**.

---

## ⚙️ What lives here

- Scenario simulations  
- Timeline projections  
- Stress tests (resource, governance, social dynamics)  
- Failure cases and edge conditions  

---

## 🧠 Purpose

To answer:

> Does this actually work when reality pushes back?

---

## 🧪 Important

A simulation is successful if it reveals flaws.

Not if it “looks good”.

Failure here prevents collapse later.

---

## 🌊 Constraint

If simulations consistently fail:

→ The system must change  
→ Not reality  

---

## 💙 A small reminder

You don’t build the future by believing in it.

You build it by **testing it honestly**.
