# CODE OF CONDUCT  
## The Covenant Against Existential Violence

The Manifesto of Existential Sovereignty is founded on the principle that **every human being is an Irrevocable Sovereign**.  
Our community is built on Flow, trust, and the elimination of fear.

This Code of Conduct outlines the ethical obligations of all contributors, ensuring that our digital Node remains a **Soul’s Sanctuary (Refugium Anima)** and a space free from the logic of Mammon.

---

## 1. OUR SOVEREIGN STANDARDS

We expect all contributors to uphold the following standards in every interaction  
(Issues, Pull Requests, Comments, and Discussions):

- **Existential Respect**  
  Treat all individuals as Sovereign Creators.  
  No personal insults, harassment, or verbal attacks based on identity, background, or ability.

- **Non-Violent Communication**  
  Use inclusive and clear language.  
  Avoid sarcasm, aggressive language, or condescending tones that induce fear or shame.

- **Focus on Flow, Not Identity**  
  Criticize ideas and proposals — never the person presenting them.  
  All discussions must aim to improve the Manifesto or the principles of `C_global`.

- **The Honesty of Mistakes**  
  We uphold the *Principle of Honoring Mistakes*.  
  Honest errors are protected.  
  Intentional sabotage or malicious deception is a violation.

---

## 2. UNACCEPTABLE CONDUCT  
### (Existential Violence)

The following behaviors constitute **Existential Violence** and will not be tolerated:

- **Targeted Harassment**  
  Stalking, intimidation, or sustained disruption of discussions.

- **Existential Threats**  
  Any language implying that a person’s worth or right to the **Guaranteed Baseline**  
  is conditional on performance, productivity, or opinion.

- **Doxxing and Exposure**  
  Publishing private information (name, location, personal details) without explicit consent.  
  This violates the principle of **Distributed Ignorance**.

- **The Logic of Mammon**  
  Promoting scarcity, competition, financial dominance, or exploitation of others.  
  Flow is built on abundance and shared sovereignty.

---

## 3. ENFORCEMENT AND LEX FINIS  
### (The Ultimate Consequence)

Violations of this Code are treated seriously and addressed swiftly.

Our enforcement model prioritizes **rehabilitation over punishment**,  
in alignment with the *Rebirth Mandate*.

### Reporting
If you experience or witness a violation, report it to the Project Maintainers  
(the Rotating Council).

> Contact: *elinor.frejd@proton.me*

### Consequences

1. **First Violation**  
   A private written warning, referencing the breached principle.  
   The individual is encouraged to enter a brief *Soul’s Sanctuary*  
   period of self-reflection.

2. **Second Violation**  
   Temporary ban (7 days) from all project repositories and discussion channels.

3. **Severe or Repeated Violations — Lex Finis**  
   Permanent removal from the project.  
   This **Existential Exile** exists solely to protect the integrity and safety of the Node.

---

By contributing to this Manifesto, you agree to uphold this Code of Conduct  
and to participate in the creation of a world defined by dignity, trust,  
and existential freedom.
