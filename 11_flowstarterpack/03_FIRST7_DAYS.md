# 🚀 First 7 Days — Start Flow Without Calling It Flow

**Day 1**: If you can, find 2–5 people. Ask: “What is hardest right now?”  
**Day 2**: Choose ONE small shared action, something to make the day easier (share meals, fetch water, childcare, share tools).  
**Day 3**: Do it once.  
**Day 4**: Repeat + adjust.  
**Day 5**: Add a second small action.  
**Day 6**: Create a simple rotation, take turns.  
**Day 7**: Sit together and reflect: What became easier?

**If people feel**:
- **Less** stress  
- **More** time  
- **More support** 

They will naturally continue.  
This is Level 0. 

**If conflict arises**: Pause. Breathe. 

Ask: 'What happened in your body when that occurred?' 

Listen without fixing. 

If you can't resolve it now, let it rest. 

Return tomorrow. Conflict is normal. Rupture doesn't mean failure.

**And, if you're alone right now**: 

Start with Day 1 for yourself. What is hardest? 

Choose one small action that makes YOUR life easier today. 

Rest. Breathe. 

The circle will come. *For now, you are enough*, exactly like this.
