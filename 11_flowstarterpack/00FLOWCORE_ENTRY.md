## The Simplest Possible Explanation of Flow

(This is your true “Start Here” file)

Flow is a way of living where:
- Everyone has Baseline (food, water, shelter, healthcare)
- No one is coerced
- Rest is infrastructure
- Governance rotates by lottery (LOTUS)
- Children have voice
- People organize in small Circles (3–30 people)
- Systems grow slowly, safely, voluntarily

Flow is not a commune.  
Not socialism.  
Not capitalism.  
Not charity.  
It is **collective sufficiency**, built step by step.

---

## 🌱 How Flow Actually Begins

Flow starts with three things:

1. **Rest**  
   You don’t have to earn the right to exist.

2. **Trust**  
   One person you can sit with without fear.

3. **One shared action**  
   Something that makes life easier today.

That’s it.  
Everything else **grows** from these three seeds.

---

## 🌀 The First Step (Always the Same)

Ask someone you trust:

> “What is hardest in your daily life right now?”

Then ask:

> “What is one small thing we can make easier if we do it together?”

Choose one:
- Share a meal  
- Fetch water together  
- Watch children together  
- Share tools  
- Cook together  
- Clean together  
- Walk together  
- Rest together  

Do it once.  
Then again.  
Then adjust.  
This is Level 0.

## 🪜 The Path (Short Version)

Flow grows in phases:

**PHASE 0** — Recognition
You see the system clearly. You map your Baseline.

**PHASE 1** — Mapping
You list your skills. You find 2–5 people.

**PHASE 2** — Parallel Building
You keep your job.  
You build Flow alongside it.  
You reduce one dependency every 3 months.

**PHASE 3** — Threshold
If you stopped earning today, would you starve?  
If no → hybrid or partial exit is possible.

**PHASE 4** — Early Flow
You detox from productivity guilt.

**PHASE 5** — Integrated Flow
You contribute because you want to.  
You become a bridge for others.
