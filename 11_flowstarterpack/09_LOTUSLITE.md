# 🌼 LOTUS Governance (Short)

LOTUS = Lottery, Open, Turn-based, Uncentralized, Safe.

- Leadership rotates by random draw  
- No one holds power long enough to capture it  
- Circles decide by consensus  
- Transparency is default  
- Children have voice  
- Rest is protected  
- Harm is addressed through restoration, not punishment  

**LOTUS prevents hierarchy before it forms.**
