# 🏙️ Flow in Urban Informal Settlements (Condensed)

Urban ≠ rural.  
No land. High density. High rent. High violence.

What Works:
- Sack gardens (greens, herbs)
- Rooftop rainwater (if allowed)
- Biosand filters
- Shared solar charging stations
- Housing cooperatives (rent reduction)
- Women-only circles for safety
- Invisible organizing (low visibility)
- Youth-led projects (they see what adults miss)

What Doesn’t:
- Large gardens  
- Livestock  
- Open political organizing  
- Visible structures that attract police or strongmen  

Start with:
1. Rent  
2. Water  
3. Safety  
4. Small income projects
