
📖 Flow Glossary (Short)

**Baseline** — unconditional survival (food, water, shelter, healthcare).  
**Circle** — 3–30 people who support each other.  
**LOTUS** — rotating governance by lottery.  
**Refugium Anima** — rest and emotional safety.  
**L × S × I** — Lugn / Calm × Spontanitet / Spontaneity × Inkännande / Empathy.  
**Hybrid** — living partly in Mammon, partly in Flow.  
**Node** — 30–500 people with shared infrastructure.
