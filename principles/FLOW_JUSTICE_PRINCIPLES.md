## PURPOSE

> "Justice is not punishment. Justice is repair."

Justice exists to:
- **Repair harm** (restore what was broken)
- **Prevent recurrence** (change systems, not just individuals)
- **Protect community** (safety without cruelty)

---

## CORE PRINCIPLES

### 1. Harm Exists on a Spectrum

**Flow categorizes harm into four levels:**
- **Level 1: Friction** (Minor conflicts, resolved through dialogue)
- **Level 2: Disruption** (Moderate harm, affecting community function)
- **Level 3: Serious Harm** (Psychological damage, physical injury, sabotage)
- **Level 4: Unforgivable Harm** (Violence with intent to kill/maim, sexual violence, child abuse, sabotage of Baseline)

---

### 2. Restorative Justice (Default Framework)

**Questions asked:**
- What harm occurred?
- What needs were unmet?
- How can we repair the harm?
- What systemic changes prevent recurrence?

---

### 3. Unforgivable Harm Protocol (Level 4)

**Unforgivable Harm = permanent expulsion.**

**Process:**
1. **Immediate Separation**
2. **Investigation**
3. **LOTUS Tribunal**
4. **Decision:** Permanent expulsion if clear evidence.
5. **Exile:** Flow-ID deactivated. Baseline still provided elsewhere.

---

### 4. Sanctions (Progressive Intervention)

**Sanctions are NOT punishment. Sanctions are intervention.**
- **Level 1:** Process Correction
- **Level 2:** Autonomy Restriction (Governance pause)
- **Level 3:** Infrastructure Co-Management
- **Level 4:** Node Suspension (Temporary expulsion)

---

### 5. Baseline Never Revoked (Even for Harm)

**No matter what someone did, they get Baseline.**
- Survival is not a reward.
- Desperation causes more harm.
- Exceptions destroy the principle.

---

### 6. Victim-Centered Justice

**Victims decide how much they engage.**
- Forgiveness is NOT required.
- No-contact orders are enforced.
- Refugium Anima (trauma support) is always provided.

---

### 7. Perpetrator Accountability Without Dehumanization

**Person who caused harm is still human.**
- We reject "monster" framing.
- Accountability + Support = Possibility of change.

---

### 8. Systemic Analysis (Root Causes)

**Every harm reveals systemic failure.**
- After accountability, we ask: "What protocol or culture contributed to this?"
