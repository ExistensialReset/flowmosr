# ⚖️ Flow Justice
## Repair without domination

This folder defines how harm is addressed in Flow.

Not through punishment.  
Not through authority.  

But through **accountability, repair, and protection**.

---

## 🧭 What this is

Justice in Flow is not about control.

It is about:
- Restoring safety  
- Addressing harm  
- Preventing repetition  

---

## ⚖️ Core direction

> No one is disposable.  
> But harm cannot be ignored.

Both must be held simultaneously.

---

## 🧩 What lives here

- Accountability protocols  
- Harm response systems  
- Mental health integration  
- Boundary and separation processes  

---

## 🚫 What does NOT exist

- Punitive systems for control  
- Power-based judgment  
- Permanent exclusion without cause  

---

## 🧠 How to think about justice

Not:

“Who is guilty?”

But:

- What happened?  
- What is needed now?  
- How do we prevent this again?  

---

## 🌊 Important

Some situations require separation.

Flow does not deny this.

But separation is:
- Protective  
- Contextual  
- Not identity-based  

---

## 💙 A small reminder

Justice is not about winning.

It is about:

> Making it possible for life to continue without harm.
